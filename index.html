June 2023

你好，我是Joe，目前生活在美麗的台灣。我已退休，內人Joyce將於2030年退休，我們決定於2031年開啟一個前所未有的冒險 - 環遊世界。

這個決定從2023年的某一天開始萌芽。當時，我們坐在家裡的客廳，看著一對中國的年輕朋友，開車從中國前往歐洲自駕遊。那一刻，一個驚人的想法浮現在我們的腦海：為什麼我們不自己試試呢？

這個想法似乎瘋狂，但也充滿了刺激和無限的可能。我們開始研究各種旅遊目的地、路線規劃、和必要的資訊，包括簽證、汽車設備、預算，甚至是語言技能。我們知道這是一場龐大的挑戰，但正是這種挑戰讓我們如此興奮。

這個網站將記錄我們旅行前的奮鬥，準備工作，以及在旅途中的每一步。我們將分享我們的夢想、計劃、以及成就，希望這個網站能成為啟發其他人勇敢追尋自己夢想的地方。

請留意我們的網站，並加入我們的冒險旅程！

Hello, I'm Joe, currently living in the beautiful island of Taiwan. I have already retired, and my partner Joyce is set to retire in 2030. We've made a decision to embark on an unprecedented adventure in 2031 - a journey around the world.

This decision began to sprout on a certain day in 2023. At that time, we were sitting in our living room, watching a pair of young Chinese friends driving from China to Europe on a self-drive adventure. In that moment, an astonishing idea crossed our minds: why not try it ourselves?

The idea might seem crazy, but it's filled with excitement and limitless possibilities. We began researching various travel destinations, route planning, and essential information, including visas, car equipment, budgets, and even language skills. We knew it was a monumental challenge, but it's precisely these challenges that filled us with excitement.

This website will document our pre-travel struggles, preparations, and every step along the journey. We will share our dreams, plans, and accomplishments, hoping that this website becomes a place of inspiration for others brave enough to pursue their dreams.

Please stay tuned to our website and join us on this adventurous journey!
